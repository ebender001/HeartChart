Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a case?

Start by selecting the option to add a new case within the app. You can then draw CABG grafts on the heart diagram and add any additional procedures before saving or exporting the case.

How do I generate a PDF?

After completing the case diagram and procedural details, select the PDF option to generate a shareable case summary.

How do subscriptions work?

HeartChart offers a yearly auto-renewing subscription that enables saving cases within the app. Subscription management, billing, and cancellation are handled through your Apple App Store account settings.

I found a bug. What should I do?

Please email support with a description of the issue and any screenshots that might help reproduce the problem.
